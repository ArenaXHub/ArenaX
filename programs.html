Programs

ArenaX currently focuses on badminton as its primary sport due to its accessibility, low infrastructure requirements, and suitability for community-based participation.

Nature of Activities

  • Structured training sessions
  • Community practice programs
  • Local-level tournaments and friendly matches
  • Beginner and amateur engagement initiatives

ArenaX Sports & Event Management is a registered MSME engaged in sports development, training programs, and community-based sporting initiatives.
