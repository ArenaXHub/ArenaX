About ArenaX / Our Purpose

ArenaX Sports & Event Management is a professionally managed MSME focused on promoting grassroots sports participation through structured, community-based badminton programs.

In addition to youth-focused initiatives, ArenaX encourages wider community participation by engaging amateur and recreational players across age groups, creating inclusive sporting environments that support lifelong physical activity.

ArenaX Sports & Event Management is a registered MSME engaged in sports development, training programs, and community-based sporting initiatives.
