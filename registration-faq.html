Questions Before You Register

How many matches will I play?

Every participant plays multiple matches. The format ensures adequate performance assessment and not single-match elimination.

Is this knockout or league format?

This is not a simple knockout. ArenaX uses a structured assessment format combining match play and skill evaluation.

Is Mixed Doubles available?

Based on registrations and suitability, ArenaX may introduce additional pairing formats.

How long does the event take?

The event is typically conducted within one day. Reporting time and schedules are shared in advance.

Is this for beginners?

ArenaX is designed for developing and competitive players. The focus is skill recognition, not just winning.

Will everyone get a certificate?

Yes. Every participant receives a performance-based certificate, not a participation certificate.
