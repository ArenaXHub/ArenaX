Certificate Verification

ArenaX Performance Certificates are issued only after verified on-court evaluation conducted during official ArenaX programs.

Each certificate includes a unique Certificate ID that enables authenticity verification and confirmation of performance details.

How to Verify a Certificate

  • Locate the Certificate ID printed on the certificate
  • Email the Certificate ID to verify@arenax.in
  • Or contact ArenaX through official phone or WhatsApp

Upon verification, ArenaX confirms:
• Event name
• Participant rank
• Skill category
• Performance band
• Date of issue
